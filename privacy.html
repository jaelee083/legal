Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 8, 2026

Plain is a minimalist journaling application designed to help you write one calm, private note each day. We built Plain with a single principle in mind: your thoughts should remain yours.

Data We Collect

We collect no data.

All journal entries are stored exclusively on your device using on-device storage. Plain has no servers, does not send any information over the internet, and has no technical ability to access your notes.

How Your Data Is Used

Your notes exist only for you. They are never transmitted, analyzed, or shared with us or with any third party.

Third-Party Services

Plain does not use analytics, advertising, crash reporting, cloud services, or any other third-party data collection tools.

Data Storage & Security

All content is saved locally using Apple’s on-device storage frameworks. Because nothing leaves your device, the strongest form of privacy is built in by design.

Your Control

  • You can edit or delete any entry at any time.
  • You can permanently delete all your data from within the app.
  • You can export your entries as plain text whenever you wish.

Children’s Privacy

Plain is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will revise the “Effective Date” at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the way Plain handles data, please contact us at privacy@plain.app.

This policy applies to the Plain iOS application.
